Our Journey

Key milestones in TechSphere's evolution from startup to industry leader.

Founded TechSphere

June 2015

Sarah Johnson launches TechSphere as a personal tech blog while working at Google, focusing on making complex tech concepts accessible.

First Podcast Episode

March 2017

TechSphere Podcast launches with an interview featuring a former colleague discussing cloud computing trends.

First Full-Time Hire

January 2018

Michael Chen joins as CTO, allowing TechSphere to expand beyond blog content into interactive dashboards and tools.

1M Monthly Readers

October 2020

TechSphere reaches major traffic milestone during pandemic as more people turn to online tech education.

Series A Funding

May 2022

Secures $8M in funding to expand team and develop new interactive learning platforms.
